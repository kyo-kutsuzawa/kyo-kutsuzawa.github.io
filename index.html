Research

Movement generation with neural networks

Robotic applications may extend to our daily tasks like cooking and cleaning. These tasks often have various constraints, priorities, and situations. This research aims to address this issue with sequence-to-sequence models, a kind of neural networks, that can generate complex time series by extracting latent variables.

Force-signal processing

Robots will contact the environment not only directly but also through grasped objects and tools. This research aims to estimate the tool shape and contact force on the tool.

Robot control for tool use

Robotic tool use is an approach for actualizing the versatility of robots as humans do. This research aims to demonstrate its effectiveness and the controller design methods.
